## Full description  (≤ 4000 chars)

```
RecipeCrave is your cooking companion for everyday meals and feel-good eating. From a 30-second meal-planner to a pantry-matched recipe engine, it takes the guesswork out of "what's for dinner?" — and pairs every dish with optional food-as-medicine insights when you want them.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Discover real recipes — Thousands of tested recipes spanning Italian, Indian, Thai, Mexican, Mediterranean, and modern American cuisines. Filter by diet (vegan, keto, gluten-free, dairy-free, paleo, low-FODMAP), by course, by total time, by ingredients on hand.

• Match the pantry — Tell RecipeCrave what's in your fridge and we'll surface recipes you can cook tonight without a grocery run. The pantry-matcher prioritises perfect matches first, then "missing 1–2 ingredients" close calls.

• Generate a meal plan — One tap turns "I want a healthy week" into a full Mon–Sun plan with shopping list. Lock the meals you love; swap the ones you don't.

• Cook with herbs intentionally — Browse our food-as-medicine vertical: ginger for nausea, turmeric for inflammation, garlic for immunity. Every claim is sourced; every page reminds you it's culinary guidance, not medical advice.

• Scale + convert without tabs — Built-in calculators handle servings (resize recipes 1× → 12× live), unit conversion (cups ↔ grams), recipe cost, calorie estimates, ingredient substitutions, baking ratios, and storage life.

• Reads offline — Open RecipeCrave with no signal and your last-viewed recipes still load. Install to your home screen for a full-screen, app-like experience.

• Daily inspiration — Optional push notifications send a 09:00 daily digest of seasonal recipes, herbal tips, and new how-to guides. One tap to unsubscribe.

PRIVACY-FIRST BY DEFAULT

No ads. No third-party trackers. Anonymous analytics only. Newsletter signup is optional and you can delete your data anytime from your profile page. We never sell email lists.

WHY RECIPECRAVE

We built RecipeCrave because every other recipe site burns the screen with autoplay video ads and 1,500-word personal essays before the ingredients. Here, the recipe loads in under a second, the print version actually prints clean, and the food-as-medicine section is written by humans who care about evidence.

Cook better. Waste less. Feel more like yourself.

Got feedback? hello@recipecrave.com.
```

> ~2,100 chars. Leaves ~1,900 chars of headroom for adding feature bullets after each major release.
